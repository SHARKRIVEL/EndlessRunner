## Overview
Endless Runner is a fast-paced 3D running game where the player moves forward continuously and must avoid dynamically generated obstacles while collecting items to survive longer. The game focuses on quick reactions, increasing speed, and strategic use of power-ups.

As the player covers more distance, the game gradually becomes faster and more challenging.

---

## Core Gameplay
- Continuous forward movement with limited player control
- Dodge obstacles appearing on the ground and in the air
- Collect coins and apples to increase score and survivability
- Speed increases after covering certain distances

---

## Major Features
- **Player Movement**
  - WASD for movement
  - Limited forward and backward movement for fine control
  - Slide ability for dodging low obstacles

- **Dynamic Obstacle System**
  - Ground walls, air walls, and stones appear randomly
  - Obstacles vary in size and position
  - Collision with obstacles reduces player health

- **Health & Power System**
  - Player starts with three health points
  - Collecting three apples restores one health
  - Collecting twenty apples activates a special power ability

- **Power Mode**
  - Activated via a button once charged
  - Allows the player to pass through walls without losing health
  - Power lasts for up to 10 seconds

- **Scoring System**
  - Coins increase the playerâ€™s score
  - Encourages risk-reward gameplay

- **Difficulty Scaling**
  - Player speed increases after traveling certain distances
  - Makes survival progressively harder
